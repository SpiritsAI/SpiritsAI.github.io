About Us

SpiritsAI is an unofficial, independent AI discussion group initiated by undergraduate students from South China University of Technology. We are not affiliated with or endorsed by SCUT in any official capacity. Our community is dedicated to sharing cutting-edge knowledge, technology, and applications in the AI field, providing a platform for students interested in AI to exchange ideas and learn together.

Whether you are a newcomer to AI or an experienced developer, you can find like-minded partners here to explore the unlimited possibilities of AI together. Our activities are organized by students, for students.

Though members will leave SCUT, the exploration of AI will never end.
